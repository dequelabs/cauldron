## Examples

### Notice Component

The `Notice` component is used to display a message to the user. It can be used to display information, warnings,

<Note>
  If your section needs to have an accessible name, be sure to include an
  `aria-label` or `aria-labelledby` pointed to the `id` of your label.
</Note>

```jsx example
<Notice type="info" title='Information'>
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Aliquam at porttitor sem. Aliquam erat volutpat. Donec placerat nisl magna, et faucibus arcu condimentum sed.
</Notice>

<Notice type="caution" title="Caution">
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Aliquam at porttitor sem. Aliquam erat volutpat. Donec placerat nisl magna, et faucibus arcu condimentum sed.
</Notice>
```

### Notice With Composed Contents

```jsx example
<Notice>
  <div className="Notice__title">
    <Icon type="star" />
    <h2
      style={{
        margin: 0,
        padding: 0,
        fontSize: 'var(--text-size-small)',
        fontWeight: 500
      }}
    >
      You are a star!
    </h2>
  </div>
  <p style={{ margin: 'var(--space-half) 0 0 0' }}>
    Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Aliquam at
    porttitor sem. Aliquam erat volutpat. Donec placerat nisl magna, et faucibus
    arcu condimentum sed.
  </p>
</Notice>
```
